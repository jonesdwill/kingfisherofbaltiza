Three weeks to go. This trip has been a long time in the making, and the final stretch is very much underway. We are writing this from the middle of a month-long boatyard frenzy at Johnson and Loftus in Ullapool — a few members of the crew have recently graduated, and now Kingfisher is getting the spotlight treatment. Most waking moments are consumed by thoughts of the KF, but everything is coming together and we are incredibly excited.

If the next few weeks go to plan, we leave Ullapool for Graah's Havn on the East Coast of Greenland on **10th July**. Our goal is to climb new routes in one of the most remote and rarely visited mountain areas accessible by sea.

Read the full expedition overview — route, crew, climbing plans, and logistics — on the **[Greenland 2026 page](/greenland-2026)**.

We will post updates here as the trip progresses.
